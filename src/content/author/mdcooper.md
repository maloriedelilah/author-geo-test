M. D. Cooper writes the Aeon 14 universe — hard military science fiction and space opera spanning the fall of the Sol System, the generation-ship voyages that followed, and the centuries of interstellar civilization that came after. This test build features two corners of that timeline: **Origins of Destiny**, Tanis Richards' early career as a Hegemony intelligence officer, and **The Intrepid Saga**, the century-spanning colony voyage that turns her into a legend.
