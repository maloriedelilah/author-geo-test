Origins of Destiny is the prequel arm of the Aeon 14 universe, set in the InnerSol and Sol System reaches of the Terran Hegemony before the launch of the *Intrepid*. Reading order runs from the prequel novella *Storming the Norse Wind* through *Tanis Richards: Kill Shot*.
