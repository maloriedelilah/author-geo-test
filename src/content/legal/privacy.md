This Privacy Policy explains what information this website collects, why, and
what rights you have over it.

## Who we are

This site is operated by the author identified on the [About](/about) page
("we", "us"). Contact details for privacy questions are listed at the bottom
of this page.

## What we collect

- **Newsletter signup (if offered on this site).** If you subscribe, we
  collect the email address you provide, and pass it to our email service
  provider for the sole purpose of sending you updates you asked for. We do
  not sell or rent this information.
- **Standard hosting logs.** Like almost every website, our hosting provider
  automatically logs basic technical information (e.g. IP address, browser
  type, pages requested) for security and reliability purposes. This site
  does not run its own analytics or advertising trackers beyond what its
  hosting provider requires to operate.
- **Cookies.** This site does not set marketing or analytics cookies. Any
  cookies present are strictly those required for the site or its hosting
  provider to function.

## Legal basis for processing

Where GDPR applies, our legal basis for newsletter emails is your **consent**
(Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you may withdraw at any time by unsubscribing.
Hosting logs are processed under **legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f)) in
keeping the site secure and operational.

## Who we share it with

- Our **email newsletter provider**, solely to deliver the newsletter you
  signed up for.
- Our **hosting/CDN provider**, solely to serve the site and keep it secure.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties
for advertising purposes.

## How long we keep it

We keep newsletter subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed, and
delete it within a reasonable period after you unsubscribe. Hosting logs are
retained only as long as our hosting provider's standard operational window.

## Your rights

If GDPR (or a similar law) applies to you, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restrict or object** to certain processing.
- **Port** your data to another service.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (e.g. by unsubscribing from the newsletter).
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

## International transfers

Our email and hosting providers may process data outside your country of
residence. Where required, such transfers rely on appropriate safeguards
(such as Standard Contractual Clauses) provided by those services.

## Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect
personal data from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Updated" date at the top of
this page reflects the most recent revision.

## Contact

For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at the email
address listed on the [About](/about) page.
